ZOO MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Zoo Management System is a web-based technology that manages and organizes various tasks and activities associated with proper functioning of Zoo. This website will be developed in such a way that it is both user and admin friendly. The user can only check zoo related information like details about the zoo and the animals present in zoo along with its description. Admin is the one who is responsible for many activities like generating tickets for the visitors visiting the zoo, managing animal details and ticket prices for each category (child/adult) and can also view history of tickets booked in a particular period of time. Overall, a Zoo Management System is an essential tool for modern zoos. It helps them to manage the animals easily and exhibits more efficiently, while also providing a better experience for the vistors.
